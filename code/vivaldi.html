Antonio Lucio Vivaldi was born on March 4, 1678, in Venice, IT, to Giovanni Battista Vivaldi and Camilla Calicchio. He was baptized immediately after birth, likely due to his poor health or a supposed earthquake that day, which may have been misremembered as occurring on the same day. Two months later, the usual baptismal ceremonies were performed. Vivaldi had five siblings, and his health was fragile, with symptoms of "tightness of the chest" that may have been a form of asthma. Despite these challenges, Vivaldi learned to play the violin and compose music, although he was unable to play wind instruments.
